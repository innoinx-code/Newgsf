About Gulab Seva Foundation

Gulab Seva Foundation is a charitable trust committed to transforming lives. We provide support, guidance, and opportunities to those who need it most — the jobless, orphaned, elderly, and underserved families. Our work is driven by humanity, compassion, and the belief that everyone deserves a chance to live with dignity.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We aim to build an India where every person has a safe home, every child receives education, every elderly person is respected, and every deserving youth receives meaningful employment. Vulnerable people receive care and support without discrimination.

Our Vision

A society where no one is hungry, homeless, or helpless. Communities grow together with humanity as the greatest strength. The first 2 Crore followers receive unique GSF IDs, representing trust, belonging, and priority access.
